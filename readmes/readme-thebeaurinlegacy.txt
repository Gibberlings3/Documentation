-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
										General Description: 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-Welcome to my first NPC mod - The Beaurin Legacy - for Baldur's Gate Shadows of Amn! 

-The mod includes:
	1. 2 Original Joinable NPCs (one is harder to get!) 
	2. Beautiful Custom Portraits
	3. Friendship Track
	4. Numerous Banters and Interjections
	5. Encounters and Quests
	6. Voicing for both NPCs (Minyae is fully voiced)
	7. Romance with a Bioware NPC (our favorite Red Wizard!)
	8. Player-Initiated Dialogues
	9. Custom Friendship Track Theme


*Minyae can be found in front of the Crooked Crane in the City Gates of Athkatla.
*Depending on PC's level, Minyae's experience may be 161000, 400000, 800000 or 1200000 experience points.
*If you experience any problems with Minyae's or the hidden NPC's select/confirm sounds, select her in the game and press the "K" key. This should fix the problem.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
										How To Install:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Download and extract the zip file in your Baldur's Gate II folder.
2. Double-click "Setup-TheBeaurinLegacy.exe" to start the installation.

*To reinstall or uninstall the mod, run Setup-TheBeaurinLegacy.exe in your game directory.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
										Frequently Asked Questions:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q1: Where can I find Minyae?
A1: She can be found when you enter the Crooked Crane. She should be near the bookshelves.

Q2: Is there a romance with Minyae?
A2: No, but she does romance a Bioware NPC.

Q3: Is there any special places/requirements for Minyae's friendship talks?
A3: There is a player-initiated friendship talk, and there are talks after certain encounters and quests.

Q4: Is there any NPCs that I should take with me or avoid?
A4: No need to avoid any NPCs, but I would highly recommend taking Edwin with Minyae.
