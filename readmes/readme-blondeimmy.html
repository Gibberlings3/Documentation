Blonde Imoen

A Gibberlings Three Mod
Version 4
Author:

This mod allows Imoen to have lovely, blonde hair. Ok, I can't guarantee its lovelyness but it is blonde! You have the option of giving her short, blonde hair (Normal Imoen length) or longer blonde hair (Aerie-ish length).

Compatibility

This mod is designed to work with Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn with or without the Throne of Bhaal expansion pack.

Installation

Windows
Blonde Imoen is packaged and installed with WeiDU and is distributed as a self-extracting archive. To install, simply double-click the archive and follow the instructions on screen.

Alternatively the files can be extracted from the archive using WinRAR. If properly extracted, you should have a BlondeImmy folder, BlondeImmy.tp2 and Setup-BlondeImmy.exe in your BG2 folder. To install, simply double-click Setup-BlondeImmy.exe and follow the instructions on screen.

Please run Setup-BlondeImmy.exe in your BG2 folder to reinstall, uninstall or otherwise change components.

Mac OS X
If the mod was previously installed, uninstall it before extracting the new version. Please note that this mod is incompatible with Mod Organizer. Blonde Imoen is packaged and installed with WeiDU. To install, simply extract the contents of the mod into your BG2 folder. If properly extracted, you should have a BlondeImmy folder, BlondeImmy.tp2, Setup-BlondeImmy, and Setup-BlondeImmy.command in your BG2 folder. To install, simply double-click Setup-BlondeImmy.command and follow the instructions on screen.

Contents

General component related blurb.

Blonde Imoen
This component will change Imoen's avatars to have blonde hair and will change her portrait to that of Imoen with short blonde hair.

Long Hair for Blonde Imoen (Requires Blonde Imoen)
This component requires the first component to be installed. It will change Imoen's avatars to that of an elven mage (who has long hair) and will change her portrait to that of Imoen with long blonde hair. A side effect of this component is that she will becomes shorter (as elves are shorter than humans).
