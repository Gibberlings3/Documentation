Sword Coast Stratagems Component: Potions for NPCs

The algorithm used is as follows:

Healing potions:

  • L4-5: 1/3 chance of a healing potion, 1/6 chance of two, 1/6 chance of an extra-healing potion, 1/3 chance of nothing.
  • L6-7: 1/2 chance of an extra-healing potion, 1/6 chance of a healing potion, 1/6 chance of two, 1/6 chance of nothing.
  • L8+: 1/3 chance of two healing potions, 1/3 chance of an extra-healing potion, 1/3 chance of two extra-healing potion.

Magic-protection potions:

  • L7-8: 1/5 chance of a potion of freedom, 1/5 chance of a potion of invulnerability, 3/5 chance of nothing.
  • L9-10: 1/6 chance of a potion of freedom, 1/6 chance of a potion of invulnerability, 1/6 chance of a potion of magic protection, 1/6 chance of a potion of magic shielding, 1/3 chance of nothing.
  • L11+: equal chances of a potion of freedom, a potion of invulnerability, a potion of magic protection, or a potion of magic shielding.

Battle potions (warriors only):

  • L5-6: 1/2 chance of one of: hill giant strength, heroism, speed, defense.
  • L7-8: 1/9 chance each for each of stone giant strength, hill giant strength, heroism, defense.2/9 chance for speed. 1/3 chance for nothing.
  • L9+: equal chances of each of: frost, fire or cloud giant strength, speed, power, heroism.

In addition, the following have particular choices preselected:

  • Desreta (the entropy-obsessed fighter-thief in the BG docks), Riggilo (the thief in Durlag's Tower), and Slythe get three Invisibility potions each.
  • Zargal (the hobgoblin bandit in the SW wilderness) gets two healing potions.
