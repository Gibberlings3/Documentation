Sword Coast Stratagems Component: Dark Side-Based Kobold Upgrade

Basically, some of the kobolds get a one-use script which causes them to spawn several more kobolds (the exact number is randomised and keyed to chapter). Among the newly-spawned kobolds are "guards" (who wield poisoned daggers). In addition, a kobold ambush force is spawned close to the exit to L3 of the mines--it includes a kobold chieftain and a couple of shamans.
