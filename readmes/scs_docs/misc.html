Sword Coast Stratagems Component: Improved Miscellaneous Encounters

The full list is:

  • Laurel the paladin's gibberling hunt. As it stands this is a ridiculous encounter - the "horde" of gibberlings is tiny. I've quadrupled their number - it's still not a difficult fight, but it's a more realistic one.
  • The Cloakwood druids. They're a pushover to a well-played party - each gets a couple of levels and some apprentices.
  • Tamoko and Bassilus. These solo priests are also pushovers - they gain a few levels (they're still not that tough).
  • Drasus's party. The party guarding the entrance to the Cloakwood mine gains a couple of levels.
  • The Red Wizards of Thay each gain three levels (they don't get any new spells in their CRE files, but the AI will pick up on their level and adjust their defences accordingly).
  • Cythandria, Sarevok's paramour, is currently guarded by a pair of hopeless ogres; they're replaced by Stone Golems.
  • Slythe the assassin gains a level (which brings his backstab into quadruple-damage territory). He also slugs a potion of invulnerability, and receives a Non-Detection spell, before the battle starts.
  • The Iron Throne assassins you meet in the Undercity each gain a couple of levels (making this party a pretty serious threat, actually).
