The Tweaks Anthology - Unique Icons

A comparison of the icon updates for Unique Icons component of Tweaks Anthology. Last updated for v12.

Note that each section includes all possible options, e.g. the Baldur's Gate section covers everything from the original Baldur's Gate without expansions through BG2EE, so not all icons will be swapped on all platforms. Items which already have a unique icon--either from another mod, or one of the items that get a new icon on the Enhanced Editions--will be skipped automatically.
